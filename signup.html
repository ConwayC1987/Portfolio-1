Newgrange Winter Solstice Lottery Draw

People gather at Newgrange for winter solstice dawn on each of the mornings from December 19th to December 23rd inclusive. Sunrise is at 8.58am. All access to the chamber is decided by lottery. However, everyone else is welcome to come and stand on the outside of the monument.

Names are drawn for each of the solstice mornings, each person drawn can bring a guest to attend on a specific morning. Some additional names are also drawn and placed on a reserve list. The reserve list is there in case someone whose name is drawn for the initial list is not contactable or else finds it impossible to travel to Newgrange on the date they have been assigned.

Lottery winners cannot offer their place in the chamber to someone else. There are typically about 30,000 entries for Solstice Lottery.

Applications can be made by email to the Office of Public Works.
